" Colemak remapping for movement keys
noremap m h
noremap n j
noremap e k
noremap i l

" Movement keys remap for visual mode
noremap vn vj
noremap ve vk
noremap vi vl

" Movement keys remap for insert mode
inoremap <C-m> <C-h>
inoremap <C-n> <C-j>
inoremap <C-e> <C-k>
inoremap <C-i> <C-l>

" Movement keys remap for command-line mode
cnoremap <C-m> <C-h>
cnoremap <C-n> <C-j>
cnoremap <C-e> <C-k>
cnoremap <C-i> <C-l>

" Movement keys remap for window navigation
nnoremap <C-w>m <C-w>h
nnoremap <C-w>n <C-w>j
nnoremap <C-w>e <C-w>k
nnoremap <C-w>i <C-w>l
